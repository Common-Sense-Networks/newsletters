HAPPY LEAP DAY!
It only happens every four years—how will you spend it?
 

Below are some fun leap day activities to do with kids:

LETTER to Future Self

Have them write a letter (or dictate a letter to you) to their future self to open on the next Leap Day on February 29, 2028! Encourage them to describe themselves now and express what they would like to learn or do in the next four years. Then, seal it in an envelope, write the child’s name on the outside, and place it somewhere for safe keeping.

EXTRAordinary Acts

Talk to your child about the concept of "extra" and how they can use this “extra” Leap Day to do something kind for someone else—a sibling, friend, teacher, or classmate.

"Leap" Frog SNACKS

Ingredients:

  • Green grapes
  • Sliced cucumber
  • Cream cheese or peanut butter (mindful of allergies)
  • Raisins or blueberries

Instructions:

  • Use a cucumber slice for the frog’s face
  • Place 2 green grapes for the body
  • Add two raisins or blueberries for the eyeballs
  • Use cream cheese or peanut butter to help stick together
