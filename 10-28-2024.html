Trick-or-Treat Safety Tips

🎃  Decorate costumes and bags with reflective tape ✨ or stickers and, if possible, choose light colors. 💡

 

👻 Opt for face paint 🎨 or makeup 💄 instead of masks, as masks can obstruct your child’s vision. 

 

🎃  Have kids carry glow sticks or flashlights 🔦 to help them see and be seen by drivers. 🚘

 

👻 When selecting a costume, make sure it is the right size to prevent trips and falls. 🙌🏽
 

Halloween Matching Game

Print and play this spook-tacular Halloween matching game.

Printable includes two sets: 9 larger squares designed for younger kids and 30 smaller squares for older kids!

PREP

  • Print two double-sided sets of cards (preferably on cardstock).

  • Cut out cards, ensuring are all the same size (to avoid giving clues during gameplay). If you aren’t able to print double-sided, you can print as separate sheets and tape or glue the cards together.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Lay the cards face down on a flat surface.

  • Take turns flipping over two cards at a time to find matching pairs.

    • Find a match? Keep the pair and go again.

    • No match? Flip cards back over in the same spot.

  • Player with the most pairs at the end wins!

EXTEND THE LEARNING

Halloween Memory Game Printable

Invite kids to share what they notice as they compare the cards each turn. Encourage them to think about differences and similarities in shapes, colors, and quantities. Practice math skills by counting and comparing the amount of cards collected by each player at the end of the game.
