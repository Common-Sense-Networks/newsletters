PUFFY PAINT HEARTS

We love this sensory Valentine activity

Materials:

foam shaving cream
white school glue
food coloring

 

Prep:

Mix equal parts shaving cream and glue 

Add food coloring to create different colored “puff paint”

 

Create

Draw design on cardstock
Spread “puff” mixture

Dry for several hours
