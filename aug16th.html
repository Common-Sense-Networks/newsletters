August 16th 2022

As summer winds down, school ramps up around the country.

Here are some ideas to make the first day of school special. Who knows, some may even turn into annual traditions…
  • CREATIVE COUNTDOWN: Create a daily ceremony during which your child checks off the days on a wall calendar.
  • SCHOOL SUPPLY DRIVES: Have your child pick items they think other kids will like and assemble supplies for kids in need.
  • SPECIAL BREAKFAST: Decorate the table, tie balloons to the chairs, and serve your child’s favorite. Time tight? Prepare the night before and reheat in the morning. Or embellish cold cereal with fresh fruit.
  • SAY CHEESE: Take a picture of your child holding a sign with the grade level and year. Better yet, take a photo in the same place every year from Kindergarten to 12th Grade!

Example First Day of School Collage
  • SIDEWALK CHALK MESSAGE: Write a surprise message at the bus stop for an extra boost. Note: Consider your child’s personality to ensure this public display won’t embarrass them.
  • BUS RIDE MOTIVATION: Place a fun clip-on (compass, mini-flashlight, favorite character) on their backpack as a distraction.
  • LUNCH TIME MESSAGE: Pack a note in their lunch box. Include a joke, a photo, the first installment in a continuing story, or just say hi!

And, be sure to carve out time when they get home to hear all about their first day!
